Three-Color Scheme with an Accent Color

This page demonstrates the use of a three-color scheme, combining blue, orange, and green. The accent green color is applied to links, buttons, and certain text elements to enhance contrast and visual appeal.

Card Title

This card uses a complementary color scheme with blue and orange, and an accent green color is added to the design for buttons, links, and highlights.

Highlighted text within the card uses the complementary orange color to create contrast and grab attention.

Typography Hierarchy

The use of a three-color scheme (blue, orange, and green) creates clear visual differentiation and adds variety to the design. The green accent color provides a fresh pop against the complementary blue and orange colors.

In this design, we've used the **light blue** for the background, **dark blue** for text and borders, and **orange** for accents. The **green** is used for the buttons, links, and highlighted text, drawing the user's attention to important elements.
